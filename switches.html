Switches

Keyboard switches are the primary point of interest when building a keyboard.

They allow for customization in not just feel, but also sound.

Linear Switches

Linear switches are a favorite in the keyboard community for their signature smooth feel.

Clicky Switches

Clicky switches are known for their loud sound and signature bump-feel that very specific keyboard geeks prefer.

Tactile Switches

Tactile switches offer both the smooth and clacky sound with a bump-feel to add on top of that.
